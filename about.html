About Us

Betty Swannock’s started small in 2014 offering jazzercise classes at night in a local school gym. Small business owner and instructor Betty Swannock outgrew the premises during the first year and now runs her business out of a renovated warehouse in beautiful Mildura. She employs two extra instructors to deliver classes and takes care of the administrative side of the business. The warehouse has showers and change rooms, 5 stationary bicycles and a gym large enough for 5-10 at a time. Patrons are middle-class, middle income, mixed sexes, all ages. A typical patron pays either by the session ($10) or with a year membership ($160). Betty Swannock’s runs two sessions a day, five days a week Tues to Sat. Class sizes range from 5 to 10 per session.
